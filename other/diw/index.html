Chigiri Hyoma

69.99

Hyoma Chigiri (千切 豹馬 Chigiri Hyōma) is a contender of Blue Lock, who currently plays as a left wing for England's Manshine City during the Neo Egoist League. Michael Kaiser

Nagi Seishiro

79.99

Seishiro Nagi (凪 誠士郎 Nagi Seishirō) is a contender for the Blue Lock Project and the titular protagonist of the spin-off, Blue Lock - Episode Nagi, who currently plays as a rightwing for England's Manshine City in the Neo Egoist League. When first arriving at Blue Lock, Nagi was a member of Team V and was the best player in his team.

Reo Mikage

Itoshi Rin

Itoshi Sae

89.99

Sae Itoshi (糸師冴 Itoshi Sae) is a prodigy football player, who is known as the best player in Japan.
